Nutcracker

A nutcracker is a mechanical device used to crack open the hard shells of nuts, making them easier to eat. Traditional nutcrackers are often made of metal or wood and operate using leverage, with a lever or screw mechanism to break the shell. One of the most iconic types is the decorative wooden soldier nutcracker, commonly associated with Christmas traditions. These nutcrackers often feature a colorful, hand-painted design and double as festive ornaments, inspired by the story of The Nutcracker ballet.
